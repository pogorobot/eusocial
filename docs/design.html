DESIGN

Eusocial is meant to help you be a social creature.

There are as many ways to be a social creature as there are creatures in societies.

Where c is the total number of creatures, and s is the total number of societies,

The ways to be a social creature may be represented as sc

The ways to help a social creature be helpful may then be (sc)^2, social creatures squared

Unfortunately, not all combinatorial effects are necessarily helpful.

That is why one way to be a social creature that helps other creatures be social

is to eliminate potential ways of being by rendering them anti-social.

This is known as "setting boundaries", and is often regarded as important.

Unfortunately, existing as a social creature is the most difficult thing it is possible to do.

Fortunately, it means you're not alone.
